Alex Collado

I was born in Santa Monica CA but raised in Mexico, then moved to France and China, my inspiration comes from the world around me, mixing tradition and world flavors. Growing up close to my grandmother in Tampico, I was obsessed with the way she cooked and learned a lot from her, we cooked together for Christmas, New Years Eve or any other family reunions.Then I decided to study gastronomy in Austin, TX. I got experience working in Berlin and Poland, cooking at Dos Palillos and Casa Pablo, after that I move to San Francisco, CA where I cooked in 1 Michelin starred Spruce, then I was promoted to its sister property The Saratoga, all this was exceptionally influential and led me to seek out my own path with private chef services in the beautiful Caribbean Paradise.
